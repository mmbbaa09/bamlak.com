1.TOYOTA CELICA SUPRA MKI 1979-1981

First generation (A40/A50; 1978) The first generation of the Supra was based largely upon the Toyota Celica liftback, but was
longer by 129.5 mm (5.10 in). The doors and rear section were shared with the Celica but the front panels were elongated to accommodate
the Inline-6 instead of the Celica's 4-cylinder engine.
